The Blue Banana

A discontinuous corridor of urbanization spreading over Western and Central Europe, with a population of around 111 million. It stretches from North Wales across Greater London to the Benelux states and along the German Rhineland, Southern Germany, Alsace in France in the west and Switzerland to Northern Italy in the south.

Anatomy of the Corridor

The Core Region

The densely populated heart of Europe, characterized by highly developed infrastructure, immense economic output, and significant political influence. It encompasses global cities like London, Frankfurt, and Milan.
